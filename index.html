How to Replace the car battery

Turn off the car: Ensure the car is off and the keys are removed. Locate the battery: Find the battery under the hood. Disconnect the cables: Use a wrench to loosen the negative (black) cable first, then the positive (red) cable. Remove the old battery: Lift the battery out of the car. Place the new battery: Position the new battery in the tray. Reconnect the cables: Attach the positive (red) cable first, then the negative (black) cable. Secure the battery: Tighten any clamps or brackets to hold the battery in place. Test: Start the car to ensure the battery is working.
